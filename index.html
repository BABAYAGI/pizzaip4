EASTBROWNS PIZZA PALACE

Welcome to a family pizza place that anyone can enjoy! From family nights out to going out with your friends, Eastbrown's is the perfect place to spend any night during the week or the weekend. If you want to come have a slice, then come visit us on Garden Street in Titusville, FL. Inspired by my love for food and people, the first Eastbrown's was opened on November 26, 1983. It was built upon traditional principles and a commitment to quality, service, and long-standing relationships with communities, customers, and employees. I grew up in an Italian neighborhood where the emphasis on food making was its simplicity and flavor.
